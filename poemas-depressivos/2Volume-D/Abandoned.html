Poema

Abandoned

por Yago Primerano Arouca

I don't stand to make the wrong way
I need you too much tonight
I'm on my own tonight
No one replies to my texts
Just too sad to breathe
A voice saying "everything will be ok"
It's everything I need

I'm crying right now
Afraid of dying alone in the town
Afraid of you leave me lonely forever
I'm dying just imagining the pain

I just wanna be with you in your way
I need you too much in my life
I don't want to be on my own
Just too sad to walk
Your voice saying "I love you"
It's all I need

24 hours thinking about you
I miss you and this is killing me
Could you at least answer me one time?
A blade running down my arm
I'm afraid you to let me every day, every night
