Poema

Talk

por Yago Primerano Arouca

I just wanted to talk with you girl
I just needed to hear your voice
Hear an "I love you"

But you don't wanna talk with me
I'm so sad and I just needed you

Why did you leave me lonely?
I'm crying tonight, I wanna rip off my bones
