Poema

End

por Yago Primerano Arouca

More and more I have less time
Love, my love where have you been lying
Low and low, without you I'm dying
But this time you are far away

So I'm writing to let you know I'm dying
You haven't left my mind
Not even for a single night
Every night I regret being the guy
Who one day was your light
I stopped you from jumping from that high
