Poema

Complicate

por Yago Primerano Arouca

I'm used to it
Since I was a kid, things have always been like this
People still creating problems with useless reasons
Why so complicated?
Soon every one of us will be dead
No one will remeber us
Tell me why all of you always complicate everything
Fight for worthless teams
Defending celebrities that don't give a fuck about u
Because of fucking religions
Because of politicians that only want your money
Problems with our skin color
The problem of humanity is creating big problems for small things
They treat useless things as a priority
And forgetting what really matters and making the fucking difference
Seems like we like to live at war
War between nations war between relationships
Why do you always complicate everything?
