Poema

Anymore

por Yago Primerano Arouca

Looks like she doesn't love me anymore
She doesn't give me her hand anymore
She doesn't say "I love you more"
She doesn't ask if I'm ok anymore
When we're together she looks more at her phone
Depression is killing me but she doesn't care
I was crying and she doesn't care
