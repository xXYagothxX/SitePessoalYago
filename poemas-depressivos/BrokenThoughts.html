Poema

Broken Thoughts

por Yago Primerano Arouca

I fell in love with a stripper
But in the end she was a fucking ripper
So in your face I'mma pull the damn trigger
I cut myself when I'm lonely on my own
They never ask nothing about me because they know
That I never answer, let my blood flow
I got demons on my ears
I got future plans and blood tears
They don't know about me
She always cries when I leave
I'mma give her the moon so she'll stab me in the back
Some day I will never come back
She ain't gonna never come back
I can give you a rose but my heart would still be black
While I'm alive I'll give her everything
You should know that you and I don't know anything
Everything is more than our limited finitude can absorb
Alone on the porch smoking
I study the complexity of the universe while I think about killing myself, I'm not joking
Recently I'm seeing everything gray
I know there is no other way
Believing in a false religion is what makes them see the shine in the day
I want you to smoke me while there's time
I'mma live calculating my brushes while I die
I'mma die, I never ask for help
I can never make mistakes or cry
There's so much I don't know
I'll find out as much as possible while I'm alive
Isn't she beautiful?
She left me to die alone tonight
I'm kissing her while I think we'll soon be rotting underground
I'm not confident, I wasn't enough
That's why she left me to die tonight
She ain't gonna come back
I'm happy to know that no one close to me felt the kind of pain I felt
My heart is in melt
I ain't never gonna talk about it, my arm it's in melt
But still I'll find the way to your heart
I have medicine in my body, cuts in my arm and here comes the train
The time has come to end this pain
She left me to die again
Girls always leave me to die with my pain
