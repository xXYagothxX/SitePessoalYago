Poema

Lonely

por Yago Primerano Arouca

I'm a broken heart boy and a lonely guy
Watching my life escape and keeping my smile
I put the blade on my arm last night
Losing everything I love over time
But I laugh like I'm fine
Praying every night for me to die
I'll never have the life I had again, so I prefer not to be alive
Nobody knows why I cry
I never speak much, so I write
I really hate my fucking life
Just look into my eyes
They are fuckin tired
Tired of waking up wishing be dead
Everyday demons talking inside my mind
I'm dying, but don't worry honey, that's life
Don't worry cause you'll be fine
