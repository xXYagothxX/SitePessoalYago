Poema

Sea of Suffering

por Yago Primerano Arouca

In a sea of suffering
I'm all around you
I'll take you a smile
Drowned in the dubious abyss
I feel your heart
I'm the God of some
I keep you alive, but I can get you out of it
My thoughts are strategic
No day to rest
My collapse causes the destruction of the worlds

I'll forget everything
But do not forget your tears in my absence
Do not you dare say I'm better than this cocaine we smell together
