Poema

Wolves

por Yago Primerano Arouca

I'm running in circles
Forever chasing the dream of perfection
This dream is not real
And I know I will die because of it

But what the fuck do you think you are?
Who do you think you are to demand of me and make me feel worse?
Isn't enough the insatiable voice in my ear that is never quiet?
