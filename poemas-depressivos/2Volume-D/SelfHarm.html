Poema

Self Harm

por Yago Primerano Arouca

Moon in the sky, we walk with dead souls
Replace me now with all your depth blows
Living deads with a little sanity
Your room is quiet with your agony
There is blood on our hands and false cheers
My glasses blurred with all my tears
My dead eyes seeing blood coming out of your veins
Tired of these nightmares while I'm awake
Waiting for the call which one you'll never make

Please don't hurt yourself
Many people will do it for you (Hate)
Please don't hurt yourself
Dump your guilt on me, I can carry your weight
Please don't hurt yourself
Bury me coldly, you know this is my fate

I cheated everyone, I'm in a mind chain
Because I am still looking for pain
My cuts were replaced by images, it's a curse
I look around and I think it's worse
The Everest on my back
I can't be imperfect and have a crack
Never having time to breathe or err
Corpse on the table with papers, life is unfair

Please don't hurt yourself
Sorry, I tried to help but I'm useless
Please don't hurt yourself
Just remember me and don't fall into loneliness
Please don't hurt yourself
I let you hurt me instead, I'm harmless

I'm producing and working hard while you're disappeared
When I die, you'll see everything, my thoughts are uncleared
Let my memories and brain feed the bugs
Sometimes when I'm sad, you don't even give me a hug
Just do not hurt yourself when I'm dead
Because I'm always worried about you in my head
