Poema

The Beginning Of The End

por Yago Primerano Arouca

I was in a cave
I was thinking in my dark grave
Sometimes I saw the light in the end
And I just wanted to be sent
I ran every times
I always did the climbs
Just to arrive in the light
In the middle of the eternal night
But not once was real
And I didn't know how to heal
But this time seemed different
And I was ignorant
Just too late
I have tried to be straight
I ran like I never ran
More than I can
The light was approaching
I thought she was joking
I thought everything would be fine
But then came the decline
And it gone away
And everything started to be gray
Then I really fell
And I have screamed to the wall
Now I am in the darkest cave
Living on my own rage
And sometimes I still see the light
So smite
I don't know if is a hallucination
I close my eyes
And wait to my damnation
