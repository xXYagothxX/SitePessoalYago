Poema

Mi

por Yago Primerano Arouca

Man I hate when I feel like
Everyone's talking, just another night
My heart is aching but I can't speak
Again I'm feeling fucking alone

Everybody is so busy feeling at home
Why the fuck am I ever uninvited?
Maybe it's because I try too much
And it seems I'm never enough
Okay, I understand, I'm not your other friends
I would never be like any other your friend

They say I'm special
And I feel like if I die, you wouldn't even realize
Bitch today I'm so fuckin tired
I just wanna sleep above my table
I'mma die on my table tonight
