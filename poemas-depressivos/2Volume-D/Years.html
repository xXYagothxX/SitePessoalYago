Poema

Years

por Yago Primerano Arouca

Every year that passes
I see fewer faces in my front
My friends are far away
I hope you're happy wherever you are
Forget me and our moments
But just be happy

Haunted by past memories
Just don't leave me, please
I hate to be antisocial
I'm trying to give my all

Pencil in my hand
Tears in my eyes
Razor on my arm
I have to be perfect
