Poema

No Light At Night

por Yago Primerano Arouca

I make girls fall in love with me, that's not right
I break their heart because I'm not enough, I never do it right
I can't sleep because my mind flies high like the moon every night
I know I get addicted to love and cigarettes, cheap wine
At least I buy cigarettes on my side
Five days of dedication, I need to go out to get a little high
I'm quite listless and gray tonight
I look in the mirror and see two holes without light
I forgot how to feel or how not to dirty the sink tonight
People always ignore when I say something that I like
Or when I share my pains suffered for nights
I'mma sew my mouth together like
Like I've done my whole damn life
