Poema

Unanswered

por Yago Primerano Arouca

One more night awake
Waiting for a call you won't make
Sending messages, trying to call
I can't stand looking at the wall
I think I'm losing my mind
My sanity is completely blind
You treat this as a joke
Always disappears in the smoke

Why don't you pick up the phone?
Do not think that I am done
Why don't you pick up the phone?
Tonight, I am dying on my own

Let me alone to die tonight
Or give me some of your light
No matter how much I call
I will continue in the fall
I am running to you
But you're running away from me

Why don't you pick up the phone?
Do not think that I am done
Why don't you pick up the phone?
Tonight, I am dying on my own

Always an excuse
You like to abuse
My demon showing me images I don't want to see
You do not have time for me
It's fine, I will be on my own
Cause you never pick up the phone
I have a trauma, tonight I'm done
